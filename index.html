Who am I?

Hi! I'm Md Faizan, a Full Stack Web Developer from Kolkata, India. I started out experimenting with small web pages, but over time my curiosity grew into a passion for building complete applications—from clean, functional UIs to scalable backend systems and APIs.

While I enjoy designing responsive interfaces, I’m especially drawn to backend development: building APIs, handling authentication, managing data, and ensuring everything runs smoothly behind the scenes. That’s where I find the real challenge and excitement.

Some of my major projects include Weatherly (a real-time weather forecast app), ShoppyGlobe (a full-featured MERN e-commerce platform), and my YouTube Clone capstone project (video streaming with user, channel, and comment systems). These experiences have strengthened my skills with the MERN stack, authentication, file uploads, and responsive design.

Looking ahead, I aim to sharpen my backend engineering expertise while continuing to grow as a full stack developer—building applications that are not only functional, but impactful.
